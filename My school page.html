Welcome to Ethnica school and College


History


Ethnica School and College, Formally known as ethnica. An english version school under Comilla Education board. It is the only english version school in Comilla city Corporation of Bangladesh with the provision for JSC, SSC and HSC. A group of people with their philanthropic view and missionary zeal established the school in 2003 and since then the school has been serving the motto with which it has been established.
